1. Introduction

PaceMate is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Activity logs and spoon costs
  • Morning check-in fatigue ratings
  • Symptom severity ratings (fatigue, pain, brain fog)
  • Custom activities you create
  • Settings and preferences

All of this information is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or transmit this data.

Technical Information (Optional)

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version
  • Crash reports (optional, you can disable)
  • Usage analytics (optional, you can disable)

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

PaceMate does NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your contacts or social media information
  • Payment information (the app is free)
  • Biometric data

4. How We Use Your Information

Local Data Usage

Your data is used only on your device for:

  • Displaying your Energy Wallet and activity history
  • Calculating crash correlations and insights
  • Generating personalized recommendations
  • Exporting PDF reports
  • Showing notifications and reminders

What We Do NOT Do

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your health information with advertisers
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Combine your data with other users' data

5. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • iOS: iOS Keychain and local file storage
  • Android: Android Keychain and local file storage

No Cloud Storage

PaceMate does NOT use cloud storage or backup services. Your data exists only on your device.

CCPA Rights (California Users)
