Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì What is Spoon Theory?

Spoon Theory is a metaphor for managing limited energy resources when living with chronic illness. Each activity "costs" a certain number of spoons (energy units). PaceMate helps you track these costs to avoid running out of spoons and experiencing post-exertional malaise crashes.

‚ùì Is PaceMate a medical device?

No. PaceMate is a health tracking tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice and should not replace professional medical care. Always consult with your healthcare provider about your health.

‚ùì Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We never send your health information to our servers. This means your data is private and the app works offline.

‚ùì What happens if I uninstall the app?

Your data will be permanently deleted from your device. We recommend exporting your data as a PDF report before uninstalling if you want to keep a backup.

‚ùì Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export your weekly or monthly data as a PDF report from the Settings screen. These reports are great for sharing with healthcare providers or keeping personal records.

‚ùì Does PaceMate work offline?

Yes! PaceMate is designed to work completely offline. You don't need an internet connection to track your activities, log symptoms, or view your data.

‚ùì How do I customize my activities?

You can create custom activities in the Settings screen. Give each activity a name, icon, and spoon cost. You can also edit or delete existing activities.

‚ùì What conditions does PaceMate support?

PaceMate includes activity templates for ME/CFS, Long COVID, POTS, Fibromyalgia, and Migraine. You can customize templates for your specific condition or create your own activities.

‚ùì Can I sync data across devices?

Currently, PaceMate stores data locally on each device. We're exploring cloud sync options for future versions. For now, you can export your data as a PDF and share it manually.

‚ùì What's the cost?

PaceMate is available to download. We may offer optional in-app features in the future, but the core energy tracking functionality will always be available.

‚ùì How do I report a bug?

If you encounter a bug or technical issue, please email us at support@pacemate.app with details about what happened. Include your device model, OS version, and steps to reproduce the issue.

‚ùì Can I suggest features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Email us at support@pacemate.app with your feature suggestions. Your feedback helps us build a better app for the chronic illness community.
